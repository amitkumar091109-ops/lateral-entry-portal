❓ How do I apply for lateral entry positions?

To apply for lateral entry positions when they are advertised:

  1. Visit the official UPSC website (www.upsc.gov.in)
  2. Check for active lateral entry vacancy notifications
  3. Follow the official application process as per UPSC guidelines

Note: The latest lateral entry recruitment (Advertisement No. 54/2024 for 45 posts) was cancelled in 2024. Check the UPSC website for future announcements.

What is lateral entry?
Appointment of specialists from private sector/academia to senior government positions (Joint Secretary, Director, Deputy Secretary) on contract basis, typically 3-5 years.
