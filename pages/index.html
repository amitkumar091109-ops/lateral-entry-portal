What is Lateral Entry?

Lateral entry is the appointment of specialists from the private sector to senior positions in government, bringing domain expertise and fresh perspectives to public administration.

The scheme aims to augment manpower in critical areas where specialized knowledge and skills are required for effective policy formulation and implementation.
