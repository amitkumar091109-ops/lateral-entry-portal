## Scheme Context & Controversies

### Purpose of Lateral Entry
1. Bring specialized expertise from private sector
2. Fill positions requiring technical/domain knowledge
3. Modernize government functioning
4. Introduce private sector efficiency

### Criticisms & Concerns
1. **Reservation Policy:** No SC/ST/OBC reservations in lateral entry
2. **Transparency:** Selection process not fully transparent
3. **Undermining IAS:** Concerns about bypassing traditional civil service
4. **Political Nature:** Viewed as political appointments by opposition
5. **Limited Success:** Only 10 appointments in 6 years

### Political Response
- **Supporters:** BJP government, policy think tanks
- **Critics:** Opposition parties, civil service associations
- **2024 Outcome:** Scheme effectively stalled after withdrawal
