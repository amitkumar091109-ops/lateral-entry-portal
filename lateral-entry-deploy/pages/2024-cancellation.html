2024 Recruitment Cancellation

45 posts cancelled within 72 hours due to reservation policy concerns

Executive Summary

Advertisement No. 54/2024 for 45 senior positions (10 Joint Secretaries, 35 Directors/Deputy Secretaries) was cancelled on August 20, 2024, just 72 hours after publication, following intense political opposition over the absence of SC/ST/OBC reservation quotas.

72-Hour Timeline

Aug 17

Advertisement Published

UPSC publishes Advertisement No. 54/2024 for 45 positions

Aug 17-19

Political Opposition

Congress, SP, BSP, and NDA allies (LJP, JD(U)) protest absence of reservations

Aug 20

Cancellation

Minister requests UPSC to cancel advertisement; UPSC complies immediately

Core Issue: Reservation Policy

The Problem

  • No SC/ST/OBC quotas in advertisement
  • Classified as "single-post cadres"
  • Constitutional equity concerns
  • Political pressure from opposition and allies

Government's Position

  • Based on Supreme Court precedent (PGIMER case)
  • Single posts technically exempt from reservations
  • Requires comprehensive policy review
  • Parliamentary committee examining issue
